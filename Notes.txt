To assess whether these exclusions were systematic with respect to the outcome, the 10-year CHD rate among excluded observations was 
computed and found to be 21.0% (13 out of 62), compared to 15.2% in the full sample. While slightly elevated, the absolute number of excluded
CHD-positive cases was only 13, representing 2.0% of all 644 CHD-positive cases in the original dataset. This difference was
therefore considered negligible and unlikely to introduce meaningful bias. All subsequent analyses were conducted on the 
remaining 4,178 observations.
