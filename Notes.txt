Missingness analysis
-----------------------
1. CHD Rate: Glucose Missing vs Observed test
    visually, the bar plots are very close -> heart disease does not predict missing glucose.
    using chi-square testing, p = 0.2108, p > 0.05 -> not significant association
    using logistic regression (glm), we got estimate: -0.209 with p-value: 0.186 > 0.05

overall, we could say confidently that Glucose missingness is MCAR (w.r.t CHD)
